Art Director at Large

Sharon Tender

As an art director over the years, Sharon Tender has been responsible for concepting and designing advertising along with selecting and hiring photographers and illustrators. She has produced logos, brochures, web sites, direct mailings, billboards, point-of-purchase displays, bus wraps, television commercials, ad campaigns and well, you get the idea.


Some of the clients she has worked with include: Arizona Lottery, Grand Canyon Railway, Snell & Wilmer Law Offices, Arizona Department of Transportation (ADOT), eai Healthcare, G2 Office Furniture, Dairy Queen, Wilshire Grand Hotel, AAA of Arizona, Bond Engineering, Jacobs Air Water Systems (JAWS), Toddletails and TEN - The Enthusiast Network (formally known as Source Interlink Media).


One of her clients liked her work so much they kept her. For the past 10 years she has worked for TEN - The Enthusiast Network (Source Interlink Media) until they moved the offices to L.A. While at TEN she designed magazines for Mopar Muscle, Vette, Stock Car Racing, Corvette Fever along with special issues.


She is now an Art Director at Large! She really hates writing about herself. So please hire her before she starts misspelling words!
